Determining a Pan-Hantavirus CD4+ Epitope for Vaccine Constructs

This project focused on identifying CD4+ epitopes that could support the development of a pan-hantavirus vaccine strategy.

Project Motivation

Hantaviruses represent a public health concern, and broad-spectrum vaccine approaches may help address viral diversity across strains.

Approach

I used immunoinformatics-based approaches to evaluate candidate epitopes for potential inclusion in vaccine constructs.

Skills Used

Immunoinformatics, public health analysis, vaccinology, sequence analysis, and literature-based project design.

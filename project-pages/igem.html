Virginia iGEM 2025

This project focused on developing a biological device-assisted approach to enhance biohydrogen production through dark fermentation.

Project Motivation

Dark fermentation can convert organic waste into hydrogen, but production efficiency depends heavily on environmental and metabolic conditions.

Approach

Our team explored engineered biological strategies to condition the fermentation environment and improve hydrogen yield.

My Role

I contributed to team leadership, project development, modeling, experimental planning, and scientific communication.

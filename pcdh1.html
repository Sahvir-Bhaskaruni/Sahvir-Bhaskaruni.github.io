Small Molecule PCDH1 Inhibitor Design

This project focused on identifying small molecule inhibitors of PCDH1 as a potential strategy to disrupt Andes orthohantavirus binding.

Project Motivation

PCDH1 is involved in host-virus interactions, making it a potential therapeutic target for limiting viral entry or infection.

Approach

I explored candidate small molecules using computational drug design and molecular docking-based reasoning.

Skills Used

Molecular docking, drug design, medicinal chemistry, structural biology, and computational screening.
